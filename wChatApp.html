      Overview

      Connect instantly, chat effortlessly.

        Objective

        • Develop a real-time communication platform that allows users to exchange messages, media, and connect effortlessly.

        Key Features

        • Instant text, audio, and video messaging
        • Real-time notifications for new messages
        • Media sharing capabilities (images, files)
        • Group chat support for multiple users

        Technology Stack

        • Technology Stack: Flutter (Dart) for frontend, Firebase for backend and real-time messaging
        • Target Audience: Individuals and teams looking for seamless communication
        • Primary Goal: To provide a user-friendly and efficient chat platform that fosters easy and instant communication.

Project

    Delta Zone

    Defy Limits, Outrun Reality!

      Overview

      Race Beyond Limits. Defy Gravity. Own the Future

        Gameplay

        • Delta Zone is a high-speed, anti-gravity racing game where players race through complex, futuristic tracks that twist, loop, and climb.
        • Players must master unique controls like strafing and pitch adjustments to handle turns, avoid losing speed, and maximize boosts.
        • Winning requires precision, quick reflexes, and learning each track's layout to maintain momentum and edge out opponents.

        Visuals

        • The game has a vibrant, neon-lit sci-fi aesthetic with tracks set in a dystopian, post-apocalyptic Earth.
        • Environments vary widely, from deserts and icy landscapes to futuristic cities and oceans, each with rich detail powered by Unreal Engine 4.
        • Realistic lighting effects, colors, and atmospheric visuals enhance the sense of speed and the sci-fi feel.

        Modes

        • Delta Zone offers multiple modes, including a career mode, time trials, and online races, each with unique challenges and race conditions.
        • In career mode, players take on a variety of events, earning points and rewards to unlock better vehicles, upgrades, and harder tracks.
        • Time trials allow players to focus on setting personal records, while online races let players compete with others for high-stakes action.

        Multiplayer

        • The game supports multiplayer races with up to 12 players, allowing for intense and chaotic racing battles online.
        • It includes global leaderboards where players can compete for the fastest times and top ranks across the world.
